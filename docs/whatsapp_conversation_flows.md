# WhatsApp Conversation Flows

## Eligibility Test
1. **User**: "Hi, I want to take the eligibility test."
2. **Bot**: "Sure! I can help with that. Please provide your name."
3. **User**: "John Doe."
4. **Bot**: "Thanks, John! What is your age?"
5. **User**: "25."
6. **Bot**: "Great! You're eligible for the test. Let's proceed."

## Signup
1. **User**: "I want to sign up."
2. **Bot**: "Please provide your email address."
3. **User**: "john@example.com"
4. **Bot**: "Thank you! Now, create a password."
5. **User**: "mypassword123"
6. **Bot**: "Signup successful! Welcome, John!"

## Consultation Booking
1. **User**: "I want to book a consultation."
2. **Bot**: "What date would you like to book?"
3. **User**: "2026-04-10"
4. **Bot**: "At what time?"
5. **User**: "3 PM."
6. **Bot**: "Your consultation is booked for April 10, 2026, at 3 PM."

## Prescription Viewing
1. **User**: "I want to view my prescription."
2. **Bot**: "Please provide your registered email address."
3. **User**: "john@example.com"
4. **Bot**: "Here is your prescription: [link or details]."

## Medicine Ordering
1. **User**: "I want to order medicine."
2. **Bot**: "Please provide the medicine name."
3. **User**: "Ibuprofen."
4. **Bot**: "How many units would you like to order?"
5. **User**: "2."
6. **Bot**: "Your order for 2 units of Ibuprofen has been placed successfully!"
